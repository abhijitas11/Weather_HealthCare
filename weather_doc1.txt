Title: Humidity and Its Effect on Colds

- High humidity can make the air feel heavy and may worsen breathing in people with colds or asthma.
- Humidity can thin mucus, making it easier to cough up and clear congestion.
- Staying hydrated is important in humid weather to keep mucus thin.
- Use a fan or dehumidifier if indoor humidity is high.
- Avoid damp clothing and mold exposure.
